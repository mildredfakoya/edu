Education Management Information System (EMIS) for Early Childhood

Annual Education Census Questionnaire for Early chilhood

Purpose of EMIS

  • EMIS is designed to collect data related to Early Childhood Education
  • Its main aim is to facilitate informed decision-making, improve program effectiveness, and enhance the quality of education and care provided to young children.
  • Its mission is to create statistic for stakeholders, government agencies and school Administrator

Data Collection

  • The questionnaire collects insights to customize programs and support the unique needs of early childhoo
  • The Demographics page compiles questions and criteria for evaluating a school's demographics, facilities, and resources.
  • The staff profile gathers Informations related to managing and assessing the qualifications, experience, and performance of staffs
  • The children prolife collect informations in assessing and managing children enrollment and special needs care in early education
