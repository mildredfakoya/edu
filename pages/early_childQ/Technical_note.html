Technical Notes
Administrative Assistant Administrative Assistant generally provides support to centre administrator and staff to ensure effective service delivery.
Active PTA A Parent Teacher Association body which meets at least once a term
Continuous Professional Development (CPD) A structured approach to learning to help ensure competence in practice, taking in knowledge, skills and practical experience. CPD can involve any relevant learning activity, whether formal and structured, non-formal, informal and self-directed.
Daycares/Nursery Early childhood development programmes providing early stimulation for children from birth to two years.
Pre-schools Early Childhood programme providing care and education enabling children from three to five years to acquire the necessary readiness skills to access formal education.
Early Childhood Administrator The administrative head of an early childhood development or education center.
Early Childhood Development Programmes offering a structured and purposeful set of learning activities either in a formal institution (pre-primary) or as part of a non-formal child development programme for children under three years of age.
Early Childhood Education Education programme provided either within an institution specially designed for that purpose and suitable to the needs of children over 3 years of age up to 8 years of age
Early Childhood Practitioner Person employed to care for or facilitate the learning of children in daycare centres, preschools and or any other similar facility providing early childhood services.
Enrolment Capacity The number of children the building/room/space used for the early childhood services is designed to accommodate given the age of the children and the minimum child space ratio recommended by the minimum standards/CARICOM regional guidelines.
Current Enrolment Number of children enrolled at the centre as of October 31st.
Graduate Early childhood Practitioner An early childhood practitioner who has attained at least a degree in early childhood education from an accredited institution.
Purpose Built A building which is specifically built as a preschool/daycare centre
Improved water sources Include piped supply, tubewell/borehole, protected well/spring, rainwater catchment, and packaged or delivered water. Unimproved sources include unprotected well/spring, and surface water (e.g. lake, river, stream)
Improved sanitation Includes flush/pour-flush toilets, pit latrines with a slab or platform, and composting toilets. Unimproved facilities include pit latrines without a slab or platform, hanging latrines (hole over water), and bucket latrines.
Trained Early Childhood Practitioner A trained Early Childhood Practitioner is one who has successfully completed a programme in teacher education methods and early childhood teaching techniques from a recognized institution.
Observed An impairment noticed by an administrator or practitioner
Diagnosed An impairment which has been medically certfied
