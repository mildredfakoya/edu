EMIS Questionnaire for Secondary School

Purpose of EMIS

  • EMIS is designed to collect data related to Secondary-Level Education.
  • Its primary mission is to create statistics for both government agencies and schools.
  • This achievement is made possible through the use of various questionnaires that cover different aspects of education.

    • Data Collection Area

    EMIS gathers information on several critical areas:

  • Enrollment: Tracking student enrollment numbers.
  • Attendance: Monitoring student attendance.
  • Dropouts: Identifying students who leave the educational system prematurely.
  • Skills: Assessing students’ skills and abilities.
  • Sex Ratio: Analyzing the gender distribution among students.
  • Staff Information: Collecting data on school staff.
  • Subjects Offered: Documenting the subjects provided by schools.
  • Computer Literacy: Evaluating students’ digital literacy.

    • Statistical Analysis

  • The data collected through EMIS contributes to meaningful statistical analysis.
  • Schools and government entities can use these statistics for various purposes, including evaluating teaching methods and creating enabling environments for personal development.

    • Overall, EMIS plays a crucial role in informed decision-making and improving educational outcomes.
