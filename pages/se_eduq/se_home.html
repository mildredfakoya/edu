Education Management Information System

Designed to collect information about individuals with special needs in the education sector.


Questionnaire for People with Special Needs in the Education Sector.

Objectives:

Assessing Needs and Challenges:

  • Identify the specific types of special needs present among the participants (e.g., physical disabilities, neurodevelopmental disorders, sensory impairments).
  • Understand the challenges they encounter in accessing and participating in educational activities.

Understanding Support Systems:

  • Explore the effectiveness of current support systems (e.g., accommodations, assistive technologies, specialized educators) in meeting their needs.
  • Identify areas where additional support may be required.

Gauging Satisfaction and Well-being:

  • Assess the overall satisfaction levels with their educational experiences.
  • Understand how their educational environment impacts their emotional well-being and sense of inclusion.

Instructions:

  • Confidentiality: Your responses will be kept confidential and used for research purposes only. Your anonymity will be preserved, and your personal information will not be shared with any third parties.
  • Time Limit: There is no strict time limit for completing the questionnaire, but we recommend completing it in one sitting to maintain continuity and accuracy in your responses.
  • Save Your Progress: If you are unable to complete the questionnaire in one sitting, you may have the option to save your progress and return to it later. Instructions for saving and resuming the questionnaire will be provided if available.
  • Contact Information: If you have any questions, concerns, or technical issues while completing the questionnaire, please reach out to statistical officers of the Corporate Planning Unit for assistance.
  • All data furnished in this questionnaire should be uptodate .
  • The completed questionnaire should reach your District Office by the set date.
